The spirits of fallen villagers rise from the sands, pleading for justice, telling tales of betrayal and forgotten truths, asking you to be their voice and avenge their suffering.

Through secret passages and shifting sands, you discover the tomb of the forgotten prince, sealed for ages, its entrance whispering promises and warnings alike.

Final Chapter: The Secret Chamber

In the depths of the tomb, you uncover scrolls, confessions, and the royal seal—evidence of the king's cruel reign and a path to justice.

Final Chapter: Truth in the Scroll

Within a golden case lies a scroll that tells of the rightful heir, hidden and protected, waiting to return and restore balance.

Final Chapter: Trapped by Greed

As you reach for forbidden treasure, the tomb crumbles. The walls shake, and you must choose quickly: escape or perish beneath your ambition.
