Middle Kingdom
  1. (Around 2050 BC) - Reunification of Egypt under Mentuhotep II
  2. (Around 1991 BC) - Beginning of major architectural projects
  3. (Around 1985 BC) - The rise of the 12th Dynasty under Amenemhat I
1
Major Achievements of the Middle Kingdom
  1. Construction of the pyramid at Amenemhat II’s tomb
  2. Expansion of Egypt’s territory into Nubia
  3. Improvement of irrigation and agricultural systems
  4. Flourishing of literature and art
2
Important Kings of the Middle Kingdom
  1. King Mentuhotep II: Reunified Egypt
  2. King Amenemhat I: Founder of the 12th Dynasty
  3. King Amenemhat II: Famous for architectural projects
  4. King Senusret III: Known for his military campaigns in Nubia
3
Major Battles of the Middle Kingdom
  1. Battle of the First Cataract against Nubians
  2. Expansion into Nubia and the conquest of northern Sudan
  3. Campaigns to secure Egypt’s southern borders
4
