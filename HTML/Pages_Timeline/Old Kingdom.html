Old Kingdom
  1. (Around 2686 BC) - Beginning of the Old Kingdom with the 3rd Dynasty
  2. (Around 2613 BC) - The construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza
  3. (Around 2400 BC) - Peak of Egypt’s power and prosperity
1
Major Achievements of the Old Kingdom
  1. Construction of the pyramids at Giza
  2. Development of the concept of the divine kingship of the Pharaoh
  3. Flourishing of art, architecture, and religious institutions
  4. Development of a system of writing – hieroglyphs
2
Important Kings of the Old Kingdom
  1. King Djoser: Known for the Step Pyramid of Saqqara
  2. King Khufu: The builder of the Great Pyramid of Giza
  3. King Khafre: Builder of the second pyramid and the Great Sphinx
  4. King Menkaure: Builder of the third pyramid at Giza
3
Major Battles of the Old Kingdom
  1. Campaigns against Nubia and the Libyan Desert
  2. Defensive campaigns to secure the borders of Egypt
4
