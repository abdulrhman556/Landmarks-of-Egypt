Early Dynastic Period
  1. (Around 3100 BC) - Unification of Egypt under King Narmer
  2. (Around 3000 BC) - Beginning of Hieroglyphic writing
  3. (Around 2800 BC) - Start of Royal Tomb construction
1
Major Achievements of the new Kingdom
  1. Construction of the Step Pyramid by King Djoser
  2. Development of Pyramid-building techniques
  3. Completion of the Great Pyramid by King Khufu
  4. Building of the second and third pyramids at Giza
2
Important Kings of the new kingdom
  1. King Djoser: Builder of the Step Pyramid
  2. King Sneferu: First Pyramid builder
  3. King Khufu (Cheops): Builder of the Great Pyramid
  4. King Khafre: Builder of the second pyramid at Giza
3
Major Battles of the new Kingdom
  1. Battle of Sekhemkhet's reign against local tribes
  2. Defensive battles against Nubian incursions
  3. Campaigns to expand Egypt's control over Lower Nubia
4
