Sekhmet War-Healer

Sekhmet, the lion-headed goddess, was a fierce warrior and healer in Egyptian mythology. Created by Ra to punish humanity, she became uncontrollable. To stop her, Ra tricked her into drinking red-dyed beer. Calming down, she transformed into a protector. Sekhmet embodied both destruction and healing, with temples built in her honor and priests seeking her blessings.
